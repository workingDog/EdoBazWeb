EdoBaz

EdoBaz helps you travel on the Tokyo Chuo City's community bus network with confidence. Track buses in real time, find your nearest stop, browse timetables, and view upcoming departures—all from a simple, easy-to-use interface. Explore the area around each stop, switch between North and South routes, and choose the map style you prefer. Available in both English and Japanese.

Whether you’re a resident or a visitor, EdoBaz provides the information you need in a clean, easy-to-use interface for all ages.

EdoBaz does not require an account, registration, or subscription.

The app provides the following functionality:

  • View live locations of Edo Buses on the map.
  • Support for both North and South routes.
  • Browse all bus stops and their timetables.
  • Find the nearest bus stop from your current location.
  • Calculate and display the shortest walking path to the nearest bus stop.
  • Highlight upcoming departures for the next hour.
  • Choose between Standard, Hybrid, and Satellite maps.
  • Displays Look Around view (where available) to help users find the selected bus stop and its surroundings.
  • Available in English and Japanese.
